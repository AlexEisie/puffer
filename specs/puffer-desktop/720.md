# Inferred Contact Fixture Store

## Summary

The desktop fake daemon now keeps inferred contact proposals in a separate
fixture store from saved contacts.

## Design

- `contactsSnapshot` remains the saved contacts plus connector candidates
  fixture.
- `inferredContactProposals` persists proposal rows between fake daemon
  `contacts_list`, `contacts_search`, `contacts_save`, and `contacts_infer`
  calls.
- `setContactsSnapshot` can still seed proposals for tests, but those proposals
  hydrate into the separate inferred store instead of the saved contact
  snapshot.
- Saving a contact prunes overlapping proposals from the inferred store only.

## Compatibility

The fake daemon still returns the same `proposals` RPC field expected by the
Contacts screen. Browser tests can continue to seed proposal fixtures through
`setContactsSnapshot`.
