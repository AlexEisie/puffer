# GitLab Connector Catalog UX

## Design

Corbina's pipeline connector catalog includes `gitlab-webhook` in the fake
daemon fixture and exercises the same search, setup-only, runtime, and selected
details surfaces as the live catalog.

## Contracts

- Searching `gitlab merge request webhook` selects the GitLab setup preset.
- The row shows `serve` and `no trigger`.
- The details panel shows `skill:gitlab-webhook`.
- The staged command is `/connect gitlab-webhook gitlab-webhook`.
- Connector result counts account for fourteen built-in connector templates and
  seven serve-mode templates.

## Compatibility

Existing connector rows, connection rows, monitor actions, and workflow draft
controls retain their behavior.
