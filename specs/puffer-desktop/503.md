# Jira Connector Catalog UX

## Design

Corbina's pipeline connector catalog includes `jira-webhook` in the fake daemon
fixture and tests the same search, setup-only, runtime, and selected details
surfaces as the live catalog.

## Contracts

- Searching `jira issue comment webhook` selects the Jira setup preset.
- The row shows `serve` and `no trigger`.
- The details panel shows `skill:jira-webhook`.
- The staged command is `/connect jira-webhook jira-webhook`.
- Connector result counts account for fifteen built-in connector templates and
  eight serve-mode templates.

## Compatibility

Existing connector rows, connection rows, monitor actions, and workflow draft
controls retain their behavior.
