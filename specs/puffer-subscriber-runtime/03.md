# Telegram QR Login Commands

- `SubscriberCommand::TelegramQrLoginStart` starts MTProto QR login with
  optional `api_id` and `api_hash`.
- `SubscriberCommand::TelegramQrLoginWait` waits for approval of the active QR
  login and carries an optional `timeout_seconds`.
- QR commands follow the existing subscriber control-channel contract: commands
  are sent on stdin and results are emitted asynchronously as control events.
- Existing subscribers that do not implement Telegram login must ignore these
  variants and emit their normal unsupported/ignored command response.
