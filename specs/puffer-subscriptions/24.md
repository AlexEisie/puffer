# Serve Connector Catalog Entries

## Summary

The built-in connector catalog now includes existing `puffer serve` connector
crates for Discord, Matrix, and HTTP webhooks.

## Behavior

- `discord-bot`, `matrix-bot`, and `webhook` appear in connector catalog
  listings with deterministic suggested connection slugs.
- The entries are searchable from TUI and desktop connector picker surfaces.
- The entries do not claim `can_subscribe`, `can_proxy_agent`, connector
  protocol command support, or `ConnectorAct` actions.
- Workflow-trigger readiness therefore remains unavailable until those
  connectors gain a typed subscriber manifest or connector protocol stream.

## Compatibility

Existing Telegram, Slack, Lark, and email connector slugs are unchanged. The
new entries are additive catalog metadata for already-existing connector crates.
