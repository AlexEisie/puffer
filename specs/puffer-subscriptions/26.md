# Connector Runtime Hints

## Summary

Connector templates now expose derived runtime/source hints through
`connector_runtime_hints`.

## Design

- Hints are stable lower-case labels intended for selection and search UIs.
- `subscriber` is emitted only when the connector has a discoverable subscriber
  manifest through its direct slug or shared subscriber template.
- `command` is emitted when the connector has a protocol command argv.
- `internal-tool` is emitted for `puffer internal-tool ...` built-ins.
- `serve` is emitted for built-in connectors configured through `/connect` and
  then started by `puffer serve`: `telegram-bot`, `discord-bot`, `matrix-bot`,
  and `webhook`.
- `connector` is the fallback when no narrower runtime hint applies.

## Compatibility

The hints are additive metadata. They do not change trigger support, action
dispatch, auth state, or connector execution.
