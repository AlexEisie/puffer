# Connector Send Reply Target

- Connector `send_message` fallback delivery now accepts `reply_to` or
  `reply_to_message_id`.
- `reply_to` may be an integer, numeric string, or object carrying
  `message_id`/`id`; it is normalized into the subscriber `SendMessage`
  reply target.
- The generic outbound path leaves reply targeting unset because its trait
  still only exposes platform, target, and text.
